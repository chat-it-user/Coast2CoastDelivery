# SEO & Traffic Audit: Coast 2 Coast Training

## Current Issues & Why You Have No Hits

### 1. **❌ CRITICAL: No Meta Tags on Dynamic Pages**
Your Training, Delivery, About, and Contact pages have **NO custom meta tags**. They all use the homepage title and description.

**Impact**: Search engines can't distinguish pages. Users see the same snippet for every page in search results.

**Example Problem:**
- Home page: "Coast 2 Coast Training Ltd — Yacht Delivery & Training"
- Training page: Same title (should be "Yacht Training Courses - RYA Instructor")
- Delivery page: Same title (should be "Professional Yacht Delivery Worldwide")

### 2. **❌ Limited Structured Data**
Only basic LocalBusiness schema. Missing:
- Course schema (for Training courses)
- Service schema (for Delivery)
- Person schema (Jonathan's credentials)

**Impact**: Google can't understand what you offer. No rich results, no knowledge panel potential.

### 3. **❌ No Content Strategy / Blog**
No blog or content pages targeting keywords like:
- "RYA Yachtmaster training"
- "ICC certification course"
- "Yacht delivery service"
- "Sailing exam preparation"

**Impact**: You only rank (if at all) for 5 pages. No content marketing funnel.

### 4. **❌ Poor Internal Linking**
Pages don't cross-link strategically. Training page doesn't link to related Delivery page for crew positions.

**Impact**: Low link equity distribution. Users don't discover full service range.

### 5. **❌ No Analytics/Tracking**
Can't identify where traffic (if any) comes from or which pages convert.

**Impact**: Flying blind. Can't optimize for what works.

### 6. **❌ Limited On-Page Keywords**
Core keywords aren't emphasized enough:
- "Training" and "Delivery" are mentioned but not prominently
- Course names buried in translation files
- No keywords in H2/H3 headers

## 🎯 Immediate SEO Improvements (Priority 1)

### Step 1: Add Dynamic Meta Tags to ALL Pages
Each page needs unique title + description targeting specific keywords.

```
HOME:
Title: Coast 2 Coast Training Ltd — Yacht Delivery & Sailing Training
Desc: Professional yacht delivery and RYA sailing training worldwide. RYA Yachtmaster Instructor & Examiner. ICC, coastal passages, offshore passages.

TRAINING:
Title: Sailing Training Courses - RYA Yachtmaster & ICC Training
Desc: Professional sailing training courses including ICC certification, RYA Yachtmaster exam prep, and coastal passage training. Hands-on instruction by qualified examiner.

DELIVERY:
Title: Professional Yacht Delivery Worldwide - Coast 2 Coast
Desc: Expert yacht delivery services for coastal and ocean passages. RYA qualified skippers, crew positions available, berth-to-berth service worldwide.

ABOUT:
Title: About Jonathan - RYA Yachtmaster Instructor & Examiner
Desc: Meet Jonathan, RYA Yachtmaster Instructor & Examiner with tens of thousands of offshore miles. Leading sailing instructor and yacht delivery expert.

CONTACT:
Title: Contact Coast 2 Coast Training - Sailing Courses & Delivery
Desc: Get in touch for yacht training, exam preparation, or delivery services. Based in Portsmouth, serving clients worldwide.
```

### Step 2: Add Course-Specific Pages (NEW CONTENT)
Create dedicated pages for each course with their own meta tags:
- `/training/icc` - ICC Certification Training
- `/training/coastal-passage` - Coastal Passage Training
- `/training/exam-prep` - Yachtmaster Exam Preparation
- `/training/delivery-passages` - Delivery Passage Crew Positions

**Why**: Each course targets different search intents and keywords.

### Step 3: Enhance Structured Data
Add Schema.org for:
- **Course**: Each training course (name, description, instructor, price)
- **Service**: Yacht delivery service
- **Person**: Jonathan's credentials and qualifications
- **AggregateRating**: Once you get reviews

### Step 4: Emphasize Training & Delivery (Your Words)
Current copy is too generic. Strengthen keyword prominence:

**Training Page:**
- Add H2: "RYA Sailing Training Courses"
- H3 for each: "ICC Training for Sail & Power"
- Use "sailing training", "RYA instructor", "Yachtmaster exam" more

**Delivery Page:**
- Add H2: "Professional Yacht Delivery Services"
- H3: "Worldwide Yacht Delivery - Coastal & Ocean Passages"
- Emphasize "yacht delivery skippers", "offshore delivery", "crew positions"
